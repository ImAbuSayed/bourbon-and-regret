[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
A glass of bourbon, a touch of regret
You are the memory I cannot forget
Pulling you closer, feeling the heat
Making my lonely heart skip a beat

[Verse 1]
The midnight hour is settling down
Leaving behind the noise of the town
I catch the whisper of your sweet perfume
Tracing the shadows across the room

[Pre-Chorus]
Every breath is pulling me deep
Into a dream I wanted to keep
You are the spark, you are the flame
Making me softly call out your name

[Chorus]
Pour me the bourbon, leave the regret
You are the best thing that I have found yet
Losing myself in the warmth of your skin
This is the heaven I want to be in

[Post-Chorus]
Pour me the bourbon, leave the regret
[Background vocal] (Leave the regret, leave the regret)

[Verse 2]
Fingers are tracing the lines of your face
Finding my home in this beautiful place
Your gentle touch is driving me wild
Taking me back like a runaway child

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The temperature rises, the shadows collide
Nowhere to run and nowhere to hide
You are the fire, burning so bright
Taking me into the heart of the night

[Chorus]
Pour me the bourbon, leave the regret
You are the best thing that I have found yet
Losing myself in the warmth of your skin
This is the heaven I want to be in

[Post-Chorus 2]
Pour me the bourbon, leave the regret
[Background vocal] (Leave the regret, leave the regret)

[Bridge]
The silence breaks as the fire takes hold
Through shadows and light, the destiny unfolds
Whisper my name softly, Saeed tonight
[Whispered] (Saeed... Saeed...)
Letting the rhythm take all the control
An echo of truth that will never depart
Written in fate on the edge of the heart
[Echo] (Saeed... Saeed...)

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (I never knew what I was missing until you walked through that door.)
[Spoken] (Now, I just want to stay right here, lost in you.)

[Final Chorus]
Pour me the bourbon, leave the regret
You are the best thing that I have found yet
Losing myself in the warmth of your skin
This is the heaven I want to be in
